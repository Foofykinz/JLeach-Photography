Hello all! I'm Josh, born and raised in Illinois, now living in Oklahoma! I'm a photographer driven by a passion for capturing moments in all their forms—from the quiet beauty of expansive landscapes to the raw emotion found in portraits. My work spans a wide range of photography styles, allowing me to tell stories through different perspectives, lighting, and environments. Whether it's the power of a storm, the subtle details in nature, or the personality behind a face, I aim to preserve each scene with authenticity and impact.
